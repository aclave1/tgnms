Thanks for using the NANO!

NANO (Network Analyzer aNd Optimizer) is a simple, yet powerful internal tool built for the system engineers and deployment teams to quickly perform tests, analyze root causes, and provide insights in optimizing the mmWave networks.

Quick Guide
Shortcut

Press s for `summary` .

Press d for `details` .

Press t for `tests` .

Press h for `history` .

Press v for `viewer` (offline).

Press i for `about` (this page).

Search by Ctrl/Cmd + f.

Layer Control

We provide three map layers to assist deployement/visualization: Lite, Street, and Satellite. In particular, Satellite may assist to quickly visualize foliage links.

By default, the layer will show only the `overview labels` layer. It summarizes all key information. But you can also go to `history` pannel () and add additional layers of interests.
